🔒 Privacy Policy

TYF Network — The Young Foundry · Effective June 2026

Who we are: TYF Network is a safe learning community for students aged 7–14, operated by The Young Foundry. We comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023.

Data we collect: Child's name, User ID, school name, grade, date of birth, and parent email. We do not collect phone numbers, addresses, or government IDs.

How we use it: Solely to operate the platform. We never sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Parental consent: A parent or guardian must approve every child account before it is activated. Parents can reject or remove any post.

Content moderation: All posts pass through an automated content check before a parent sees them. Parents approve all creative posts before they go live.

Right to erasure (DPDP Act s.12): A parent or guardian may request full deletion of their child's account and all associated data at any time using the "Delete My Account" button in the Profile page, or by contacting the Grievance Officer below.

Data security: Passwords are never stored in plain text. All connections use HTTPS.

Grievance Officer: For any data-related concerns, email privacy@youngfoundry.in. We respond within 48 hours.

This policy may be updated. Changes will be communicated via the parent email on file.

📜 Terms of Use

TYF Network · Last updated June 2026

Who can use TYF Network: Students aged 7–14 with a parent-approved account. Parents/guardians must complete the registration and approval process.

What you can post: Your own creative work, learning experiences, summer camp stories, projects, and ideas. All posts must be original and kind.

What is not allowed: Abusive language, bullying, copying others' work without credit, sharing personal contact details (phone, address), adult content, or defamatory statements about teachers or schools.

Content moderation: All posts pass through an automated content check. Creative posts require parent approval before going live. Posts reported by 3+ users are automatically hidden pending admin review.

Parent responsibility: Parents who register a child account take full responsibility for monitoring their child's activity on the platform.

Account suspension: Violations may result in post removal or account suspension without notice. Serious violations are reported to the platform's Grievance Officer.

Grievance: Email privacy@youngfoundry.in for any concerns.

By using TYF Network you agree to these terms. They may be updated with notice via the parent email on file.
